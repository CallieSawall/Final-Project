Baking Tips & Tricks

  • Always preheat your oven before baking.
  • Use room temperature ingredients for better mixing.
  • Chill frosting before spreading for a smoother finish.
  • Practice on parchment paper before decorating for smoother application.
  • Add milk to cake box mixes to make it more dense and moist.
  • Allow the delicious creation to cool completely before decorating to prevent mess.
  • Make accurate measuremts to perfect the recipe.
  • Don't overmix dough to prevent a toughness to the goods.
